Campaign Custom Domain

Any URL that you include in your SMS / WhatsApp campaigns gets shortened to a smaller URL to reduce the number of characters in the message. Eg. A URL such as https://www.westernstateinsurance.com/policy/documents/policyname.pdf will get shortened to weurl.co/xyz in the actual SMS / WhatsApp message received by the customer. The domain of this shortened URL is always weurl.co by default. You can choose to change this domain from weurl.co to a custom domain of your choice for branding purposes. Eg. If your custom domain is wsins.com then the shortened URL will be wsins.com/xyz instead of weurl.co/xyz.

To make this change, please point your custom domain's CNAME entry to weurl.co and keep the TTL as 30 minutes. Once you've made this change, please enter your custom domain below and save the settings.
